About Us

KAASHI Cafe is a minimalist haven in the heart of Ongwediva, located at Maeurala Mall. We believe in simplicity and quality, serving carefully crafted coffees, refreshing shakes, and freshly baked pastries in a calm, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're starting your day or taking a break, we're here to provide you with a moment of peace and exceptional taste.
